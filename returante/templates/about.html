About KFC Catrey

KFC Catrey is a fast-growing food brand based in Nnewi, dedicated to producing delicious, hygienic, and affordable meals. From freshly prepared chin chin to premium shawarma and rich African dishes, we serve quality that keeps our customers coming back.

Who We Are

We are a proudly local restaurant and food production company committed to offering the very best snacks, meals, pastries, and continental treats. At KFC Catrey, we combine traditional Nigerian flavors with modern food techniques to create meals that satisfy every taste.

Our team is made of passionate cooks, caterers, and food lovers who believe in excellence and consistency. Whether it’s daily meals, events catering, or bulk production, we ensure top quality in every bite.

Our Mission

To provide delicious, fresh, and affordable meals prepared under the highest hygienic standards, ensuring customer satisfaction at all times.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading food brands in Nnewi and beyond, known for quality, consistency, and unforgettable taste experiences.
